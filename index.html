INTO THE META GAME

The future is chrome, and humanity is just hardware. In RockstarZ, you are a cyber-jockey fresh off a failed data-heist, left for dead in the gutters of Dark-City. To survive, you'll need to rebuild yourself—literally. Hunt for cutting-edge cybernetics, slot illegal bio-enhancements, and upgrade your "wetware" to become the ultimate urban weapon. In a world where flesh is a liability, how much of your humanity will you sacrifice to get to the top?

ABOUT US

TMy name is Aditya Dhanraj, and I'm the mind behind this project. As a long-time fan of the cyberpunk genre, from its classic literature to its modern-day epics, I wanted to create a space that felt truly immersive. This website began as a passion project to blend my skills in web development with my love for deep, atmospheric world-building. RockstarZ is my tribute to the genre—a world I hope you get as lost in as I did while building it.
